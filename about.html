about this place

Why I built a room
for something I never said.

This isn't a site about someone specific — it's a site about the shape a feeling takes when it never gets spoken. Everyone's carried a version of this.

the honest version

What this is, plainly

One-sided love isn't a tragedy. It's usually just a feeling that showed up early, or late, or aimed at someone who was never going to aim back — and instead of disappearing, it needed somewhere to go. This site is that somewhere: a diary, a wall of quotes, a scattering of memories, kept in the open instead of a locked notes app.

Nothing here is addressed to anyone in particular. If a page sounds like it's speaking to "you," it's speaking to whoever that word means for the reader — that's the point of writing it down instead of sending it.
